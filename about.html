About Us

Global Jigyasa is a daily news summary and quizzing app with a simple objective – keeping you abreast with the happenings of the world in the shortest possible time every day!

In everyone’s busy schedules, it is often difficult to find the time to stay updated about current affairs. This is extremely important though irrespective of your age, location, profession or ideology! Global Jigyasa app ensures that you stay in touch with daily news in just 10-15 minutes every day. You can access it while traveling to or from work, while getting ready for the day (!), or whenever you have just a little bit of free time!

The most exciting part about the Global Jigyasa app is its concept! The app has daily quizzes with 10 interactive questions. Each of these are related to the current affair happenings of the day – politics, sports, global news, economics, happy news, sad news etc etc.! The quiz is basically dependent on what’s happened in the world! Users attempt it to test their daily current affairs knowledge, and then view the answers which lead to the daily news bulletin. The bulletin has 10 simple news summaries of less than 100 words each. In this way, Global Jigyasa gamifies current affairs learning and makes the process simple and interactive.

The app also has other exciting features such as daily, weekly, and monthly quiz leader boards; and the Sunday Weekly Quiz!

Global Jigyasa has been conceptualized by Anansh Prasad, Founder and Managing Director, SkillSphere Education along with Anas Mirza, CTO of Global Jigyasa. Says Anansh Prasad, “We want individuals to enjoy learning about the world and feel challenged while doing so. Most importantly, we know that there is a dearth of time as well. Global Jigyasa addresses both of these problems and makes everyone’s life simple and enlightened!”
